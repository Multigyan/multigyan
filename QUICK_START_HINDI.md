## ЁЯТб PRO TIPS

### SEO Tips for Hindi Content
1. **Keywords**: Mix Hindi + English
   - тЬЕ "PMAY рд╕рдмреНрд╕рд┐рдбреА"
   - тЭМ Only "рдкреАрдПрдордПрд╡рд╛рдИ рд╕рдмреНрд╕рд┐рдбреА"

2. **Title Structure**:
   - Format: `[English Keyword] [Year]: [Hindi Description]`
   - Example: `PMAY 2025: рд╕рдмреНрд╕рд┐рдбреА рдФрд░ рдЖрд╡реЗрджрди рдХреА рдЬрд╛рдирдХрд╛рд░реА`

3. **Tags Strategy**:
   - 2 English tags (PMAY, CLSS)
   - 2 Hindi tags (рд╕рдмреНрд╕рд┐рдбреА, рдпреЛрдЬрдирд╛)
   - 1 mixed tag (PMAY Hindi)

### Writing Tips
1. Keep sentences short (10-15 words)
2. Explain English terms in brackets
3. Use bullet points for lists
4. Add examples throughout
5. Include official links

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
тЭМ Title only in Hindi (bad SEO)
тЭМ Too many tags (max 10, ideal 3-5)
тЭМ No translation link
тЭМ Missing alt text on images
тЭМ Content less than 300 words
